📱 MASTER PROMPT — Household Chore Splitter App (Vibe-Coded)
PART 1 — APP OVERVIEW & GOAL

You are building a mobile-first household chore app designed to make chores competitive, fun, and addictive for families.

The app is not corporate, not business-like, and not productivity SaaS.
It should feel:

Friendly

Colourful

Slightly playful

Safe for children

Still enjoyable for adults

Core idea: turn household chores into a game with points, leaderboards, rewards, and monthly winners.

The experience must feel fast, satisfying, and dopamine-driven, not slow or admin-heavy.

PART 2 — TARGET USERS

Primary:

Families with children (ages ~6–18)

Parents who want structure without constant nagging

Secondary:

Adult housemates

Couples

Shared apartments

UI must work for:

Children

Teenagers

Adults
WITHOUT feeling childish or corporate.

PART 3 — ONBOARDING & LOGIN FLOW (CRITICAL)
FIRST APP OPEN

Friendly animated splash screen

App logo animates in smoothly (bounce or fade-up)

Short tagline like:
“Turn chores into a game”

LOGIN / SIGNUP SCREEN

Include:

Login

Sign up

Demo Mode

Buttons:

“Skip for now”

“Try demo family”

Fake notifications

No long forms.
No forced account creation.
Let users explore first.

PART 4 — FAMILY CREATION FLOW
Create Family

One main button: “Create Family”

Name family

Choose theme colour (optional)

Instantly generate:

Family join link

Share sheet

Join via link

No approval required (admin can remove later)

Admin Role

Only ONE role: Family Admin

Admin powers:

Create/edit chores

Set rules (points reset period, rewards)

Manage rewards

No complex permission trees.

Daily Chores

Low points

High importance

Tidy room

High points

Confetti or burst animation

“👑 Liam just took the lead!”

Points

Past workload

Visual explanation:
“Chores balanced fairly this week”
